Paulo Castillo

Founder & AI Automation Partner

I founded vollautomatisiert.at with a simple conviction: most businesses don't need "more AI." They need their existing operations to run better — with less manual work, fewer errors, and clear accountability.

I work end-to-end with SMEs and solopreneurs: from identifying the highest-ROI bottleneck, to designing and building the automation, to embedding it into real daily operations with the guardrails that make it stick.

My focus is practical, not theoretical. Every workflow I build has defined ownership, logging, fallbacks, and human approval where it matters. The goal isn't impressive demos — it's measurable impact that survives Monday morning.

What We Believe

Reliability Over Novelty

A workflow that runs every day without breaking is worth more than a cutting-edge prototype that never leaves the demo. We build for operations, not showcases.

Diagnosis Before Prescription

We don't start with tools. We start with your business — mapping processes, finding bottlenecks, and understanding constraints before recommending any solution.

Outcomes Over Output

We don't measure success by features shipped or hours logged. We measure it by the metrics that matter to your business: time saved, errors reduced, revenue gained.
